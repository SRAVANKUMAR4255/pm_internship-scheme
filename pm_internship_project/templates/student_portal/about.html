About PM Internship Scheme

Ministry of Corporate Affairs · Union Budget 2024-25

Scheme Overview

The PM Internship Scheme is a flagship initiative of the Government of India under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, announced in Union Budget 2024-25. The scheme aims to provide internship opportunities to 1 crore youth in 500 top companies over a period of 5 years.

It bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing hands-on experience, thereby enhancing the employability of India's youth.

Benefits for Students
💰 Monthly Stipend

₹5,000 per month provided by the Government of India during the 12-month internship period.

🎁 One-Time Assistance

₹6,000 one-time grant to cover incidental expenses at the start of the internship.

🏥 Insurance Coverage

Coverage under PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana and PM Suraksha Bima Yojana.

📜 Completion Certificate

Government-recognized certificate upon successful completion of the internship.

🏢 Top Companies

Opportunity to intern at 500+ top Indian companies across diverse sectors.

👨‍🏫 Expert Mentorship

Dedicated mentors from top companies to guide you throughout the internship.
